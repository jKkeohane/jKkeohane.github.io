Geospatial Background

I built this website to illustrate some of the key studies I have undertaken during my university education at:
The University of Bristol where I studied for a BSc in Geography, where I graduated in 2021
The University of Manchester where I am studying for an MSc in Geographical Information Sciences completing the main programme of courses in May 2023.

I am currently working on my dissertation exploring geographic natural language processing and am looking forward to the first stages in my career as a geospatial scientist.
